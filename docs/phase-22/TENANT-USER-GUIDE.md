## Getting Started

### Accessing the Tenant Portal

1. **Login** to your SAGA POS account
2. Navigate to **Tenant Portal** from the main menu
3. Or go directly to: `https://your-domain.com/tenant-portal`

### First-Time Setup

When you first login:
- You'll be on a **14-day trial** (Starter plan or higher)
- Complete your business profile
- Add team members (within your plan limits)
- Explore the features

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Your Current Plan

1. Go to **Subscription** from the portal menu
2. See your current plan details:
   - Plan name and features
   - Billing cycle (monthly/yearly)
   - Next billing date
   - Current price

### Upgrading Your Plan

**Step 1:** Navigate to **Subscription** page

**Step 2:** Review available plans:

| Plan | Price/Month | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Free | Rp 0 | Small businesses, testing |
| Starter | Rp 99,000 | Growing businesses |
| Professional | Rp 299,000 | Multi-location businesses |
| Enterprise | Rp 999,000 | Large organizations |

**Step 3:** Select your desired plan

**Step 4:** Choose billing cycle:
- **Monthly** - Pay every month
- **Yearly** - Save 17% (get 2 months free)

**Step 5:** Click **Upgrade** and confirm

**What happens next:**
- Your plan changes immediately
- A new invoice is generated
- You have 14 days to pay
- Your limits are updated instantly

### Downgrading Your Plan

**Note:** Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

1. Go to **Subscription** page
2. Select a lower-tier plan
3. Confirm the downgrade
4. Continue using current plan until period ends
5. Downgrade activates on next billing cycle

### Changing Billing Cycle

You can switch between monthly and yearly billing:

1. Select your preferred cycle on the Subscription page
2. Click **Update Billing Cycle**
3. Review the price difference
4. Confirm the change

**Price Calculation:**
- Monthly → Yearly: Pay annual amount, save 17%
- Yearly → Monthly: Prorated credit applied

## Paying Invoices

### Viewing Invoices

1. Go to **Invoices** from the portal menu
2. See all your invoices with:
   - Invoice number
   - Date created
   - Due date
   - Amount
   - Status (Paid, Sent, Overdue)

### Invoice Status

| Status | Color | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Paid** | 🟢 Green | Payment received |
| **Sent** | 🔵 Blue | Awaiting payment |
| **Overdue** | 🔴 Red | Past due date |
| **Draft** | ⚪ Gray | Being prepared |

### Downloading Invoice PDF

1. Find the invoice in the list
2. Click **Download PDF**
3. PDF opens in new tab
4. Save or print as needed

### Paying an Invoice

**Method 1: Online Payment (Recommended)**

1. Find an unpaid invoice
2. Click **Pay Now**
3. Select payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Bank Transfer
   - GoPay
   - ShopeePay
4. Click **Pay Now** button
5. Complete payment in Midtrans popup
6. Wait for confirmation
7. Invoice status updates automatically

**Method 2: Bank Transfer (Manual)**

1. Download the invoice PDF
2. Transfer to bank account listed on invoice
3. Send proof of payment to support
4. Invoice marked as paid within 1-2 business days

### Payment Methods

#### Credit/Debit Card
- Visa, Mastercard, JCB
- Secure 3D authentication
- Instant confirmation
- No additional fees

#### Bank Transfer
- BCA, Mandiri, BNI, BRI
- Virtual account numbers
- Payment instructions provided
- Confirmation within 1-2 hours

#### E-Wallets
- GoPay, ShopeePay
- QR code or app redirect
- Instant confirmation
- Transaction limits apply

### Late Payment Policy

**Grace Period:** 7 days from due date

**After Grace Period:**
- Daily late fee: 0.1% of invoice amount
- Service suspension after 30 days
- Data retention for 90 days

**To Avoid Service Interruption:**
- Pay before due date
- Enable auto-renewal
- Keep payment method updated

## Support Tickets

### Creating a Support Ticket

**Step 1:** Go to **Support Tickets** page

**Step 2:** Click **New Ticket** button

**Step 3:** Fill in the form:
- **Subject:** Brief description (required)
- **Category:** Select from dropdown
  - Technical Issue
  - Billing Question
  - Feature Request
  - General Inquiry
- **Priority:** Choose urgency
  - Low: General question
  - Medium: Standard issue
  - High: Important problem
  - Urgent: Service down
- **Message:** Detailed description (required)

**Step 4:** Click **Create Ticket**

**What happens next:**
- Ticket number assigned
- Support team notified
- Email confirmation sent
- Response within SLA time

### Ticket Priorities & Response Times

| Priority | Response Time | Example |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| **Low** | 72 hours | How-to questions |
| **Medium** | 48 hours | Feature not working |
| **High** | 24 hours | Important feature broken |
| **Urgent** | 4 hours | System completely down |

### Viewing Ticket Status

| Status | Color | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Open** | 🔵 Blue | New ticket, awaiting support |
| **In Progress** | 🟣 Purple | Support is working on it |
| **Waiting Customer** | 🟠 Orange | Need your response |
| **Resolved** | 🟢 Green | Issue fixed |
| **Closed** | ⚪ Gray | Ticket archived |

### Replying to a Ticket

1. Find your ticket in the list
2. Click **View**
3. Read support's response
4. Type your reply in the text box
5. Click **Send Reply**
6. Wait for next response

### Reopening a Resolved Ticket

If the issue persists:
1. Find the resolved ticket
2. Click **Reopen**
3. Add additional details
4. Submit
5. Ticket returns to "Open" status

### Ticket Best Practices

**DO:**
- Provide detailed descriptions
- Include error messages
- Attach screenshots
- Respond promptly to support queries
- Use appropriate priority

**DON'T:**
- Create duplicate tickets
- Use "Urgent" for non-urgent issues
- Send multiple messages in a row
- Share sensitive information (passwords)

## Usage Tracking

### Understanding Your Usage

Your plan includes limits for various resources:

| Resource | Free | Starter | Pro | Enterprise |
|----------|------|---------|-----|------------|
| Users | 3 | 10 | 50 | Unlimited |
| Products | 100 | 1,000 | 10,000 | Unlimited |
| Branches | 1 | 3 | 10 | Unlimited |
| Storage | 500 MB | 2 GB | 10 GB | 100 GB |

### Checking Your Usage

1. Go to **Dashboard** or **Usage** page
2. View progress bars for each metric
3. See current value vs. limit

**Progress Bar Colors:**
- 🟢 Green: Healthy (< 70%)
- 🟡 Yellow: Warning (70-90%)
- 🔴 Red: Critical (> 90%)

### Approaching Limits

When you reach 80% of a limit:
- Warning notification appears
- Email reminder sent
- Suggestion to upgrade plan

**If You Exceed Limits:**
- Soft limit: Warning but service continues
- Hard limit: Cannot add more until limit increases
- Solution: Upgrade plan or remove unused items

### Managing Usage

**Reduce User Count:**
1. Go to Settings → Users
2. Deactivate inactive users
3. Remove former employees

**Reduce Product Count:**
1. Archive discontinued products
2. Delete draft products
3. Merge duplicate products

**Reduce Storage:**
1. Delete unused images
2. Remove old backups
3. Compress large files

## FAQs

### Billing Questions

**Q: When will I be charged?**  
A: Charges occur on your billing date (same date each month/year).

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Refunds are evaluated case-by-case. Contact support within 7 days of charge.

**Q: What happens if I don't pay?**  
A: After 30 days overdue, service is suspended. After 90 days, account may be terminated.

**Q: Can I change my billing date?**  
A: Yes, contact support to request a billing date change.

### Subscription Questions

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**  
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime. Access continues until period end.

**Q: What happens to my data after cancellation?**  
A: Data is retained for 90 days, then permanently deleted.

**Q: Can I reactivate my account?**  
A: Yes, within 90 days of cancellation. After that, data is lost.

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**  
A: Yearly billing saves 17%. Contact sales for enterprise discounts.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How do I add team members?**  
A: Go to Settings → Users → Add User. Enter email and assign role.

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: Yes, go to Settings → Export. Choose data type and format.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices.

**Q: Do you offer API access?**  
A: Yes, Professional and Enterprise plans include API access.

### Payment Questions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: Credit cards, bank transfers, GoPay, and ShopeePay.

**Q: Is online payment secure?**  
A: Yes, we use Midtrans (PCI-DSS certified payment gateway).

**Q: Can I get a tax invoice?**  
A: Yes, all invoices include tax details. Contact support for tax documents.

**Q: Why was my payment declined?**  
A: Common reasons: insufficient funds, expired card, bank restrictions. Contact your bank or try another method.
